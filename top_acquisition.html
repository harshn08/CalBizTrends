Top Acquisitions

Mergers and acquisitions occur frequently in the industry. Lot of dynamics are involved in an acquisition like organization size, industry sector and international boundary. We analyzed recent trends taking into account major acquisitions which happened in the past few years. Although majority of the acquisitions were in USA, but they are not uncommon to other parts of the world too. Following findings resulted out of this analysis:

~Times Warner Cable was acquired by Comcast Cable in 2014 for a whopping $45bn followed by the acquisition of Nextel Communications by Sprint Forest Laboratories by Actavis.
~Alltel Communications was acquired for $28bn by Verizon Communications, Inc. despite already having a well settled telecommunication business.
~ Sanofi Aventis acquired Genzyme for $20bn in France which was the biggest acquisition in terms of pure numbers out of USA.
